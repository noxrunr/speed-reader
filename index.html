Speed Reader

Instructions

Insert your text into the textarea box. Once done, press play to watch words from the text appear on the screen. Increase or decrease WPM (Words Per Minute) number to make words appear faster or slower. Also, you can skip through the words with forwards or backwards buttons.


Keyboard shortcuts:

[K] - play/pause

[J/K] - skip backward/forward by 3 words

[Up/Down] - increase/decrease WPM by 1

[ESC] - stop
